Coffee,No Milk

Palesa N Nkone
Writer Photgrapher Artist

I Am

Designer of words that envelop the mind and beguile the soul.
A manipulator of senses; you will drink my images and
suffocate on my verses. You will starve, reaching
for page after page to fill your famished core.
I am a consoler, a broken story-teller and keeper of untold secrets.
I am a creator.

I Will

Make a success of my shattered and demented self
that the world will have no choice but to reminisce
of a young black woman who stopped at nothing to
make her mark.

I Have

Lived
